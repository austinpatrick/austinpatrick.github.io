Identity - pt. 1

I like you. In fact, I really like you. At least I would if we met. I know this because I truly believe the both of us are very good for people. If you had met me four years ago, though, things would be totally different. I didn't believe this to be true about myself, and because I didn't believe it, I didn't really like myself.

The problem is that I didn't understand who I was created to be, and I definitely didn't understand what I was created to do.
